Through complex algorithms, news articles and stock price changes are analysed and weighted to give a rating indicating how confident we are that a stock price will go up or down.

The rating spans between -100 to 100, and allows you to compare different stocks.

A rating far below 0 indicates a stock that you should sell.

+3.2936

A rating close to 0 indicates that there is either not enough information, or the stock is not likely to change much.

+48.0168

A rating far above 0 indicates a stock that you should buy.
